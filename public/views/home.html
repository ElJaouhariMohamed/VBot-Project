VBot is a chatbot to help ENSA Khouribga and staff to find information easily, at the tip of their finger, it is designed to communicate effictively and practicaly with the various vistors of the website, in a fast and secure manner.

This chatbot is actually at version 1, it was trained to answer various prompts and help the users find the information they are looking for, it is capable to :

  • Chat with users on ENSA Khrouribga related subjects.
  • Provide information with high precision.
  • It is human friendly, respectful and non-biaised against any human category.
